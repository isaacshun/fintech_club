About

Our background

The University of Waterloo Financial Technology club consists of individuals who are passionate about the financial technology space, introducing the fintech space to individuals who are interested in this field, and partnering with companies to provide networking opportunities for students who are interested in joining the field.



Our vision

In the future, the UW Fintech club plans to educate and aid potential candidates who want to enter the fintech space through teaching the necessary knowledge and background of fintech, partnering with more companies to provide bigger networking opportunities, and regularly updating news and events within the fintech space.
